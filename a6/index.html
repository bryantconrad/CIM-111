Detroit

Detroit is the classic American city. The largest municipality in Michigan, it is affectionately referred to as Motor City for its contributions to the auto industry. It was ranked the worst city in the U.S. to live in for the second year in a row in 2018, beating out its in-state rival, Flint, Michigan, which took third place and doesn't even have access to clean water.

Travel Guide

Some landmarks to visit

  • Belle Isle
    • A 982-acre island park in the Detroit River between Michigan and Ontario
  • The Henry Ford
    • Largest indoor-outdoor museum complex in the U.S., with pieces of history like Thomas Edison's laboratory and the Rosa Parks bus
  • Renaissance Center
    • Huge shopping center of interconnected skyscrapers and the world headquarters of General Motors
  • Landfill
    • The hottest real estate in Detroit right now, rated by locals as the best place to live in the city
  • Drug Dens
    • The most authentic way to get a taste of life in Detroit with some of the best prices and service in Michigan
