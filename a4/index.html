The mission of Dogs and Cars is to help every owner find the perfect complement to their one and only, whether it be a dog for your car or a car for your dog.

Meet Johnny and the 2018 Nissan Altima.

Johnny's owner came to us asking for the right car to drive his beloved canine companion around in. We found him the best balance in style and comfort that works for both him and Johnny. Today, Johnny's owner is the vice president of the American Civil Liberties Union, and Johnny faithfully and comfortably rides to work with him every day.
