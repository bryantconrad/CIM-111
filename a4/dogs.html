Donny the Dalmatian
Donny the Dalmatian was born to serve. Donny knows the ins and out of fire safety procedures, and is the perfect companion for firemen. He is also a great partner for anyone who does lots of physical, outdoors work.
  • Recommended vehicle: Firetrucks, pickup trucks
  • Age: 3 years
  • Rating: 5 stars
Scottrick the Royal Corgi
Scottrick had a distinguished 4-year tenure at Buckingham Palace under the ownership of Queen Elizabeth II before retiring to become an official Dogs and Cars recruit.
  • Recommended vehicle: Horse-drawn carriage, Tesla, Rolls Royce
  • Age: 5 years
  • Rating: 5 stars
Bo the Portuguese Water Dog
Perhaps the most well-known dog on the list, Bo was famously owned by former president Barack Obama. His days in the White House are over (unless Michelle runs), and he is ready to accompany you and your car!
  • Recommended vehicle: Air Force One, amphibious vehicle
  • Age: 10 years
  • Rating: 5 stars
